Welcome to the New You!

The Circle is a unique multi discipline Health & Well Being centre on the Portslade sea front delivering everything that you need for total fitness of your mind and body.
It incorporates fitness studios, gym, complementary therapies, food and nutrition, osteopathy, hydrotherapy and podiatry. It also houses screening, monitoring, presentation and teaching facilities to help inform and provide every required discipline in the correct sequence as assessed on a 1:1 personalised basis by our trained Circle Coaches. After all, we are what we think, do and eat!
